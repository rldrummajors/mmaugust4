Marching Spotlight!

One way to get better at Marching Band is by watching other Marching Bands that possess the "next-level" skills that we are trying to obtain! Marching Spotlight features various spectacular High School Marching Bands, College Bands, DCI groups, and possibly even WGI performances!

Year: 2019

Show Name: The Bluecoats

Group: Bluecoats

Location: DCI World Championships

Experience Level: DCI

Context: The Bluecoats' 2019 production, "The Bluecoats," is a theatrical concert-style tribute to The Beatles-particularly referencing The White Album, reflecting themes of innovation, social change, and musical exploration. With the final score of 98.238, Bluecoats claimed the silver medal at DCI Finals, finishing less than a tenth point behind the Blue Devils in one of the tightest championships in recent memory.
